As the year comes to an end, I take a moment to reflect on the journey and milestones of 2025. This list serves as a brief personal record of growth, effort, and persistence.



* Top 10: Awarded the 2025 National Graduate Scholarship**

> A truly rewarding moment, and a meaningful recognition of my efforts throughout the year.



* Top 9: First Experience as an SCI Journal Reviewer

> Gaining firsthand insight into the peer-review process helped me better understand how reviewers think and evaluate research.



* Top 8: First-Author Paper Accepted by *IROS 2025*

> My first acceptance at a top-tier robotics conference, a combination of hard work and good fortune.



* Top 7: First-Author Paper Accepted by *Expert Systems with Applications*

> My third SCI paper of the year, and the only one classified as a CAS Q1 journal.


* Top 6: Responding to *Expert Systems with Applications* Reviews

> Seven reviewers, over sixty comments, and more than seventy pages of response. After finishing this, responding to reviewers’ comments started to feel as effortless as breathing.


* Top 5: First-Author Paper Accepted by *IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering*

> My first IEEE Transactions paper, an important milestone and motivation to keep moving forward.



* Top 4: Achieving an IELTS Overall Score of 7.0

> A solid result, thanks to a decent English foundation and a bit of luck. Still, receiving this score was genuinely satisfying.


* Top 3: Attending IROS 2025 and Giving an Oral Presentation

> My first in-person participation at a top international conference. It broadened my perspective and allowed me to meet many researchers I had previously only known through papers.


* Top 2: First-Author Paper Accepted by *IEEE Sensors Journal*

> My very first published paper. While not a top-tier journal, it marked an important starting point in my research journey.


* Top 1: Preparing and Submitting PhD Applications

> Several months of focused preparation went into my PhD applications this year. I sincerely hope everything works out smoothly in the coming year.
